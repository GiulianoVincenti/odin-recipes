Criolla Sauce

Description

The recipe for traditional Criolla Sauce, a classic of Argentine cuisine

The best company for meats, or for whatever you want. This Creole sauce is super easy to make and delicious!!

It is typical in Argentina, Uruguay and Peru, and its most direct variant is also known: the Criolla salad.

Ingredients

  • 1 large white onion
  • 1 small red bell pepper (or 1/2 if very large)
  • 1 small green bell pepper (or 1/2 if very large)
  • 1 small yellow bell pepper (or 1/2 if very large)
  • 2 small tomatoes
  • 1 green onion
  • 1 cup olive oil
  • 1/2 cup apple cider vinegar
  • Seasonings: salt, pepper, cumin, ground chili.

For the sauce and topping.

  • 1 onion
  • 1 can of tomatoes
  • Oregano to taste
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 12 cream cheese slices
  • 12 slices cooked ham

Steps to follow

Make in 10 minutes!

  1. SChop the onion and bell peppers very small.
  2. Remove the seeds from the tomatoes and also cut them very small.
  3. In a bowl, add the seasonings with the vinegar and stir well.
  4. Add the chopped vegetables and stir well. Add the olive oil and let it rest for at least 3 hours in the refrigerator before consuming it!
