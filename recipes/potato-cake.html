Description

A simple recipe with its won personality yo start the year well fed. Potato cake.

A classic in Argentine homes, is both a very popular dish and difficult to find in restaurants.

Our first program of the year, dedicated yo all those who follow us.

Ingredients

  • 1kg of potatos
  • 1/2kg of minced beef
  • 1 onion
  • 1/2 pimiento
  • 2garlic cloves
  • 1 broth
  • garlic powder
  • paprika
  • 25g. of butter
  • 1 splash of milk
  • nutmeg
  • oil
  • salt
  • pepper

Steps to follow

  1. Cut the potatoes into cubes and boil them with salt.
  2. Chop the onion, garlic and bell pepper.
  3. Heat the oil in a pot or frying pan and saut√© the onion, pepper and garlic.
  4. When the onion is transparent, add the meat and fry it while breaking it down with a spoon.
  5. Season with salt and pepper, add the broth, garlic powder, paprika and cook the meat for 15 minutes.
  6. Once the potatoes are boiled, mash them hot with the butter and milk. Season.
  7. Place a base of puree in a baking dish, add the meat on top (let it warm up a little) and place another layer of puree. To distribute it, wet the spoon with cold water.
  8. Bake in a strong oven or broiler for about 15-20 minutes. or until top is crispy.
  9. A chopped egg, chopped green olives or raisins can be added to the meat filling.
