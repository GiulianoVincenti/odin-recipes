Description

The Neapolitan Milanese has all the value of a strong beef Milanese added with an incredible pizza aroma.

With a topping of melted cheese, tomato sauce and the scent of oregano, this Milanese can't be beat.

Neapolitan Milanese is a national dish, very easy to prepare in a few minutes for a complete meal that few can resist.

Ingredients

  • 12 pieces of meat
  • Salt, to taste
  • 1 cup of flour
  • 4 eggs
  • 500g of breadcrumbs
  • Sunflower or olive oil

For the sauce and topping.

  • 1 onion
  • 1 can of tomatoes
  • Oregano to taste
  • Salt and pepper
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 12 cream cheese slices
  • 12 slices cooked ham

Steps to follow

  1. Salt the pieces of meat, coat them in flour, beaten egg and breadcrumbs, obtain a good breading.
  2. Fry them browning on both sides and serve on absorbent paper.
  3. For the sauce, process a small onion with the tomato and oregano, season, add the bay leaf and cook this mixture over low heat for 20 minutes.
  4. Finally, in a roasting pan, put the milanesas and on each of them a tablespoon of sauce, a slice of ham, another of cheese and gratin in a hot over until it melts.
